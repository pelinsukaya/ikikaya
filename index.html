Protective Legal Service

Protective Legal Service is a legal risk management system conducted under the supervision of specialist attorneys.

The priority is to minimize risks by examining business strategies and related tactics.

Just as companies employ specialists in financial risk management, legal risks should be managed by attorneys.

Why Protective Legal Service?

  • The judicial system in Turkey is heavily burdened and operates slowly.
  • Alternative solutions such as arbitration or settlement commissions are often insufficient.
  • Some judicial decisions may be perceived as unfair.
  • Even experienced merchants cannot be expected to know all legislation and legal risks.
  • Protective legal service prevents loss of rights and transactional errors.

In short, our consultancy aims to minimize existing and potential legal risks.

System Stages

1) Confidentiality & Secrecy Liability

İki Kaya Law Firm provides consultancy services and undertakes strict confidentiality.

Our firm strictly adheres to Article 36 of the Attorneys’ Act.

Article 36 of Attorneys’ Act: Attorneys are forbidden to disclose any information obtained or entrusted to them.

2) Examination

Existing risks are identified by examining the company’s structure and relationships.

All contracts and relationships are reviewed; risks are analyzed.

3) Regulatory Procedures & Contract Management

Existing contracts are analyzed and revised to minimize legal risks.

We do not offer ready-made solutions; we provide tailored contract services.

4) Strategic Legal Consultancy for Top Management

We provide legal input at the strategy planning stage.

5) Sustainability & System Supervision

We monitor compliance and ensure adaptation to changing conditions.
